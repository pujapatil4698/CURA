Quizzards

If you know what is happening around the world, then you are at the right place!

Kaun Banega Crorepati? Well, not Crorepati but we can make you Hazarpati. On a serious note, if you know what is happening around the world, then you are at the right place. With world we mean Business, Sports, Entertainment, Current Affairs, History and basic General Knowledge. Show your mettle in this event and surely no one can stop you from being a Hazarpati.

Round 1 (Written round) :
  • 1. All the participants will be given some riddle based questions in a Google form, which they have to answer and submit online (on Unstop).
  • 2. First round quiz will be conducted between Feb 4th and Feb 6th and would be time bound. Based on the scores, participants will be shortlisted to round 2.
Round 2- General Quiz Round with the shortlisted candidates (Final round) :
  • 1.This will be in online mode. Quizzes will be conducted in different sections.
  • Section1- Business Quiz Round.
  • Section 2- Personality Round (Who am I)
  • Section 3- Buzzer Round (if possible using Hands up feature in zoom)
  • The Round 2 will take place over Zoom call where all the participants have to keep their camera and mic on during the whole Quiz session.
Questions :
  • 1. All the questions will be decided in collaboration with the students of the organizing team and faculties.
  • 2. The further details will be communicated to the candidates shortlisted from round 1.
General guidelines :
  • 1. Team size(2-3)
  • 2. Utmost authenticity and discipline is expected from the teams.
  • 3. The decision of the organizing committee will be binding.
