Finance Workshop

Ever wondered how an investor should think? How can we ensure adequate life insurance, reasonable medical insurance cover…. How to design an accurate financial planning… Yay!! You are at the right place..

The School of Management, National Institute of Technology, Warangal in collaboration with the Association of Mutual Funds in India is coming up with an intriguing finance workshop on “Personal Financial Planning and Wealth Creation through Mutual Funds” to help us in embarking the journey of wealth creation through sustained investments which is most essential for prosperity.



The workshop will be conducted on an online platform on 18th February, 11AM-1PM.. The further details regarding the (online platform, workshop link, whatsapp link) will be communicated to the registered ones. The participants will get an e-certificate.
