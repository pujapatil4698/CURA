About NITW

     National Institute of Technology, Warangal (Deemed University), earlier known as Regional Engineering College, was founded in 1959.
     On October 10, 1959, Pandit Jawaharlal Nehru laid the foundation stone for this institute, the first of 30 NITs (previously known as RECs) in the country. The Institute is renowned for its committed teachers, staff, and cutting-edge facilities that support a positive learning environment. The Institute never stops aiming for higher standards of technical competence. Our interests include developing a socially relevant curriculum that is also internationally accepted, utilizing cutting-edge teaching techniques, and emphasizing the students' overall growth. UNESCO and the UK's help in the past allowed for the completion of numerous developmental projects. The World Bank's assistance under the Technical Education Quality Improvement Programme (TEQIP) from 2004 to 2009 came in handy for the Institute's overall development.
     The Institute currently houses thirteen academic departments and a few advanced research centers in a variety of engineering, purely scientific, and management disciplines, as well as close to 100 laboratories set up in a distinctive pattern of operation. A large stadium, an indoor games complex, a central library with cutting-edge amenities, a student activity center, a mega computer center, seminar rooms with the necessary infrastructure, a dispensary with cutting-edge amenities, etc. reputable faculty, excellent student community, exceptional technical and support staff, and a strong administration have all helped to cement N.I.T., Warangal's position as a leader.
     The Institute offers 29 post-graduate programs (M.Tech., M.Sc., MCA, MBA) in engineering, sciences, and management, as well as research programs in engineering, sciences, humanities, physical education, and management. It also offers eight undergraduate engineering programmes (B.Tech.). The institute is renowned for its training programmes for teachers and business professionals as well as its research and development, industrial consulting, and continuing education activities.
     Along with roughly 269 Ph.D. scholars (150 Full Time and 119 Part Time), N.I.T., Warangal is a residential institute with over 3355 (UG: 2251 & PG: 1104) students. It contains 16 residential buildings, including one hostel for international students, two hostels for ladies, one mega, and one ultra-mega hostel for boys. There are five separate messes for boys, one mess just for girls, and one mega mess with modern amenities for boys, foreign students, and girls. In addition to the employees who are hired through outsourcing, it employs 360 administrative and support workers and about 200 teaching staff. The 248 acres of gorgeous wooded terrain are home to a self-contained residential campus. It is situated on both sides of the Hyderabad-Warangal highway, 3 km from Kazipet Railway Station and 12 km from Warangal Railway Station, respectively. The distance between it and Hyderabad is 140 kilometers. In the state's cultural and historical landscape, Warangal occupies a special place. The Warangal Fort, Thousand Pillar Temple, Badrakali Temple, and Ramappa Temple have all been points of interest due to their beautiful and detailed architecture. Other attractions for students in the area include lovely gardens, wildlife sanctuaries, lakes, and rocks. With the exception of a few hot summer days in May and June, the area has a pleasant climate all year round.
     The institute is a residential campus with all the necessary amenities for the employees and students to reside there. The following statistics data might provide some insight into the campus buildings. Administrative Building (3,331 sq m), Academic Buildings (34,448 sq m), Library Building (3,610 sq m), Students Hostels (32,642 sq m), Sports Pavilion and Gallery (784 sq m), Director's Bungalow, Staff Quarters, and Guest House are among the buildings on the campus that occupy the plinth space (31,598 sqm.) The campus has a 12-kilometer-long road network. More land is now being created for the various uses mentioned above and for expanding the road network.
     The institute offers one of the best placement services in the nation. The institute's graduates are in continual contact with the institute and hold respectable and admirable positions all over the world. Every new student who enters our school is eligible to take part in the rich tradition and heritage that are specific to NITW. NITW has 11th place among the nation's technical institutes, according to the 2015 Outlook Survey.
